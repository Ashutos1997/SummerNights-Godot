  SUMMER NIGHTS — Game Summary & Architecture Overview
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

GAME OVERVIEW
─────────────
Summer Nights is a vibrant 3D arcade shooter built in Godot Engine 4.7.
Armed with a high-powered water gun, you stand on a tropical beach island 
framed by palm tree groves, drifting low-poly clouds, and a warm ocean 
horizon. Your goal: cool down the relentlessly hot Sun by blasting it 
with water before the summer heat overwhelms you!

As you cool the Sun across 5 scaling levels of difficulty, the sky shifts 
dynamically from a scorching deep red through golden sunset orange to a 
refreshing dusk blue. 

GAMEPLAY & MECHANICS
────────────────────
- Water Cannon Firing: Left Mouse Button fires a continuous water spray stream.
- Crosshair & Aiming: Virtual mouse crosshair with customizable mouse sensitivity.
- Water Tank Management: Water tank drains while spraying and recharges when idle.
  If empty, you must wait until it recharges to 25% before shooting again.
- Solar Heat Regeneration: Sun heat constantly regenerates over time and scales 
  faster with each progressive level.
- White-Hot Solar Heat Vents (Critical Spots):
  * White-hot core & fiery orange rim craters spawn on the Sun's surface every 4.5s, 
    shooting a continuous white steam geyser into the sky.
  * Hitting the vent triggers 2.4x Critical Cooling (24°/s), plays sizzle audio, 
    and flashes the HUD crosshair golden-white!
- Solar Flare Ember Interceptions:
  * Every 9-12s, the Sun launches a low-poly fiery ember cluster in a 4-second arc 
    toward the beach.
  * Spraying a flare mid-air for ~0.33s cools and shrinks it down until it explodes 
    into steam, awarding a +30% Water Tank Refill!
- Victory & Level Progression:
  * Win screen overlay appears on level completion.
  * 5 levels of scaling difficulty, leading to a Game Complete victory sequence.

VISUAL STYLE & ENVIRONMENT
──────────────────────────
- Low-Poly 3D Sun Model: Sketchfab CC0 low-poly sun with warm emission materials.
- Procedural Sky Shader: summer_night_sky.gdshader featuring dynamic gradient shifts, 
  animated background clouds, and horizon glow.
- Drifting 3D Low-Poly Clouds (CloudLayer.gd): 18 procedural low-poly cloud clusters 
  drifting left-to-right with sine bobbing and randomized scale variations.
- Circling 3D Low-Poly Seagulls (SeagullLayer.gd): 4 stylized seagulls circling 
  high over the ocean horizon with wing-flapping and gliding animations.
- Dynamic Wind Sway: Organic multi-axis sine wave animation swaying palm tree 
  canopies and tropical bushes in the summer breeze.
- Heat Haze Shader (heat_haze.gdshader): Screen distortion overlay adding warm atmospheric shimmer.
- Stylized Ocean Water Shader: Scrolling ripple normals with real-time ReflectionProbe.
- 3D Water Gun & Recoil: Low-poly 3D Blaster with subtle, accessible recoil physics.

HUD & ACCESSIBILITY (WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA)
─────────────────────────────────────
- High-contrast HEAT bar (top center) & WATER bar (bottom center).
- LVL label (top left) & Crosshair synced to mouse movement.
- Settings Screen: Adjust Mouse Sensitivity, Master Volume, SFX Volume, Music Volume, 
  and High-Contrast / Reduce Motion accessibility modes.
- Credits Screen: Complete attribution for 3D models, shaders, fonts, and SFX.
- Accessibility Compliance: Minimum 4.5:1 / 7:0:1 text contrast ratios, high-contrast 
  outlines, expanded 44x44px touch targets, and full Reduce Motion support (WCAG 2.2.2).
- Single-Key Dismissal: ESC key or ← BACK button closes Settings / Credits overlays seamlessly.
- Root Viewport Loading Screen (LoadingScreen.tscn): Eliminates grey screen flashes and 
  double loading transitions.

  TECH STACK & ARCHITECTURE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ENGINE & RUNTIME
────────────────
  Godot Engine 4.7.1 (stable)
  Rendering:      Forward+ (Metal 4.0 / Vulkan)
  Target FPS:     60fps (Native Apple Silicon & Intel Universal Binary)

PROCESSED & PROCEDURAL ARCHITECTURE
───────────────────────────────────
  - GDScript: All gameplay logic, HUD management, environment generators, 
    and 3D entity handlers.
  - Performance Optimization: 128x128 procedural noise textures for instantaneous 
    scene loading (< 0.05s) with full lush jungle foliage density.
  - Export Ready: Pre-configured export_presets.cfg for macOS (.app / .zip) 
    and Windows Desktop (.exe).
  - 0% GenAI: 100% hand-crafted shaders, low-poly procedural Godot meshes, 
    Kenney UI assets, and CC0 open-source files.
